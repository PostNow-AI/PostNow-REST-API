## ✅ GRUPO 1: Teste (3 Estilos Base)

### 1. Minimal Clean
```
Create professional Instagram post mockup (1080x1080px):

STYLE: Ultra Minimalist (Apple/Muji aesthetic)

LAYOUT:
- Background: Pure white (#FFFFFF) with very subtle texture
- Content area: Centered, 600x600px maximum
- Margins: Minimum 150px from all edges

CONTENT:
- Single element: Either minimal text or simple product silhouette
- Typography: Ultra-thin sans-serif (Helvetica Neue UltraLight style)
- Font size: Large (80-100pt for heading)
- Text color: Pure black (#000000) or very dark gray (#1A1A1A)

COMPOSITION:
- 90% negative space (white)
- 10% content (centered element)
- Perfect symmetry
- Breathing room everywhere

AESTHETIC:
- Premium, high-end, sophisticated
- Less is more philosophy
- No gradients, no shadows, no effects
- Professional product photography vibe
- Think: Apple product launch, Muji catalog

QUALITY:
- Clean, crisp, professional
- Instagram-ready square format
- High contrast for mobile viewing
```

### 2. Corporate Blue
```
Create professional Instagram post mockup (1080x1080px):

STYLE: Corporate Professional (IBM/Microsoft/LinkedIn)

LAYOUT:
- Top 30%: Navy blue header (#1E40AF)
- Bottom 70%: White content area
- Clear horizontal division

CONTENT:
- Header: White text, company-style title
- Main: Professional business photo OR clean data visualization
- Typography: Corporate sans-serif (Helvetica/Arial style)
- Professional stock photo: Business meeting, handshake, or modern office

COLORS:
- Primary: Navy blue (#1E40AF)
- Secondary: Medium blue (#3B82F6)
- Accents: Gray (#64748B, #334155)
- Background: White (#FFFFFF)

COMPOSITION:
- Structured grid layout
- Aligned elements (left or center)
- Organized, clean, no chaos
- Rule of thirds

AESTHETIC:
- Trustworthy, credible, authoritative
- Fortune 500 company style
- Serious business tone (no playfulness)
- LinkedIn/Corporate America vibe
- Think: IBM ads, Microsoft presentations

QUALITY:
- Professional stock photography
- Clean typography
- Business-appropriate
```

### 3. Bold Colorful
```
Create professional Instagram post mockup (1080x1080px):

STYLE: Bold Vibrant Energy (Spotify/Nike/Airbnb campaigns)

LAYOUT:
- Full bleed: Edge-to-edge color
- Dynamic composition (not centered)
- Asymmetric, energetic flow

CONTENT:
- Background: Vibrant gradient (hot pink #EC4899 → orange #F59E0B)
- Visual: Abstract shapes OR energetic action photo
- Typography: Extra bold, chunky font
- Large impactful text (festival poster style)

COLORS:
- Maximum saturation
- Complementary contrasts
- Hot pink, orange, purple, yellow
- No pastels, no neutrals
- RGB mode, vibrant

COMPOSITION:
- Dynamic, movement-focused
- Diagonal lines, energy flow
- Overlapping elements OK
- Break grid if needed

AESTHETIC:
- Youth culture, street art, festival vibes
- Attention-grabbing, impossible to scroll past
- Playful, energetic, fun
- Think: Spotify playlists, Nike campaigns, music festivals

QUALITY:
- High energy, vibrant
- Eye-catching thumbnail
- Works on small mobile screens
```
